About the Application

The NICU Fluid Management Application is designed to assist healthcare providers in calculating and managing fluid, feeding, and nutrient intake for neonates in the NICU.

Our application accounts for key dependencies such as gestational age, weight, and postnatal age, while balancing enteral and parenteral nutrition requirements. It helps manage babies from 400 grams up to 7 kg, taking into consideration various factors that influence potential intake.

Developed based on established NICU protocols, this tool aims to streamline workflows, reduce calculation errors, and optimize nutrition management for vulnerable neonates.
